📋 使用说明

  • 来源：本词库综合整理自2021-2025年六级听力真题（新东方、CSDN、B站等平台的真题解析），涵盖长对话、短文理解、新闻报道三种题型。
  • 核心逻辑：六级听力答案选项几乎不直接出现听力原文原词，而是通过同义词替换、词性转换、句式变换来改写。识别这些替换关系是答对题目的关键。
  • 排列方式：原文高频词 → 选项中的替换词，附真题语境说明。
  • 四大替换模型：①词性转换（名词↔动词↔形容词） ②近义词替换 ③短语替换 ④句式转换/语义概括

🟣 六、句式转换 / 语义概括型替换 占比约10%
⚠ 此类型最具隐蔽性：六级听力中约40%的答案需要语义概括。原文用一整句话描述，选项用总结性词汇或不同句式表达相同含义。必须理解整句含义而非抓单个词。
原文句式 / 表达选项中的转换句式替换类型
There has been a reduction in energy consumption.→People are consuming less energy.名词→动词：reduction → consuming less
It is believed that...→People believe that...被动→主动
The film was extremely boring.→The film was incredibly dull.副词+形容词双重替换
EQ focuses on one's ability to recognize and manage emotions.→Emotional recognition and management.动词短语→名词短语概括
The ground floor of their cottage was flooded.→Their cottage ground floor was submerged.flooded ↔ submerged（2020真题）
The woman takes her own cultural norms for granted.→She is unaware of cultural differences.语义概括（2025真题）
It can process large quantities of data quickly and accurately, saving researchers' time and energy.→It can process large amounts of data efficiently.合并简化 + 同义词（2025真题）
imparting it to others→teach it to someone else正式词→口语化
socially important information→socially significant informationimportant ↔ significant
increases risk-taking→makes people more likely to take risks名词短语→从句展开
benefit→be good for / be helpful to动词→形容词短语（2026高频）
Bad driving behavior is the main reason for the high death rate on roads.→The high death rate is mainly caused by bad driving behavior.主被动语态转换（2024真题）
Most people think online learning is convenient.→Many people hold that online learning is convenient.think → hold，most → many

🎯 听力同义替换识别核心技巧

  • 模型一·词性转换：原文名词 → 选项动词/形容词（如：reduction → consuming less，important → of great significance）
  • 模型二·近义词替换：原文简单词 → 选项正式词（如：help → assist，buy → purchase，think → believe/hold）
  • 模型三·短语替换：原文短语 → 选项同义短语/单词（如：add thickness to → make thicker，without ever → never）
  • 模型四·语义概括：原文具体描述 → 选项概括性表述（如：quarrel a lot → persistent arguments，remote work → working from home）
  • 实战技巧：听音时关注"词族"——听到difference自动联想到different/differ，听到consume想到consumer/consumption
