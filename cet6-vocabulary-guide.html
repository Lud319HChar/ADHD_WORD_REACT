第一类：中国文化与历史

传统文化 · 节日 · 文化遗产 · 历史遗迹 — 六级翻译最高频话题 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

核心词汇（描述性/通用）

专有名词 / 术语

经典翻译句子模板（7句）

1. 中国传统文化源远流长，可以追溯到几千年前。
Traditional Chinese culture has a long history, dating back to thousands of years ago.
2. 春节是中国最重要的传统节日，人们会与家人团聚，共度佳节。贴春联、放鞭炮、吃团圆饭是必不可少的习俗。
The Spring Festival is the most important traditional festival in China, during which people reunite with their families. Pasting couplets, setting off firecrackers and having a family reunion dinner are indispensable customs.
3. 京剧被誉为中国的"国粹"，融合了音乐、舞蹈、美术和武术等多种艺术形式。
Peking Opera, known as the "national essence" of China, integrates various art forms such as music, dance, fine arts and martial arts.
4. 丝绸之路促进了中国与西方国家之间的贸易和文化交流。
The Silk Road facilitated trade and cultural exchanges between China and Western countries.
5. 儒家思想深刻地影响了中国的社会结构和道德观念，强调仁、义、礼、智、信五种美德。
Confucianism has profoundly influenced China's social structure and moral values, emphasizing the five virtues of benevolence, righteousness, propriety, wisdom and trustworthiness.
6. 非物质文化遗产的保护与传承对于维护文化多样性具有重要意义。
The protection and inheritance of intangible cultural heritage is of great significance to maintaining cultural diversity.
7. 中国的四大发明——造纸术、印刷术、火药和指南针——对世界文明产生了深远的影响。
China's Four Great Inventions — papermaking, printing, gunpowder and the compass — have exerted a profound impact on world civilization.
💡 翻译技巧：文化类翻译常用 "has a long history"（历史悠久）、"dates back to"（追溯到）、"be known as"（被誉为）、"symbolize"（象征）、"inherit/pass down"（传承）、"have a profound impact on"（深远影响）。

第七类：政治与时政（新增）

国家政策 · 中国特色社会主义 · 中国梦 · 一带一路 — 近年考频上升 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

核心词汇

专有名词 / 术语（官方标准译法）

经典翻译句子模板（5句）

1. 改革开放以来，中国发生了翻天覆地的变化，综合国力显著增强。
Since the reform and opening-up, China has undergone earth-shaking changes, with its overall national strength significantly enhanced.
2. "一带一路"倡议促进了沿线国家的基础设施建设和经济发展。
The Belt and Road Initiative has promoted infrastructure construction and economic development in countries along the routes.
3. 中国政府坚持走和平发展道路，推动构建人类命运共同体。
The Chinese government adheres to the path of peaceful development and promotes the building of a community with a shared future for mankind.
4. 实现中华民族伟大复兴的中国梦，是全体中国人民的共同愿望。
Realizing the Chinese Dream of the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation is the common aspiration of all Chinese people.
5. 中国坚持中国特色社会主义道路，全面推进中国式现代化建设。
China adheres to the path of socialism with Chinese characteristics and comprehensively advances Chinese-style modernization.
💡 翻译技巧：政治类翻译务必使用官方标准译法，不可自由发挥。常用表达："adhere to"（坚持）、"promote"（推进/促进）、"comprehensively advance"（全面推进）、"play an important role in"（发挥重要作用）。

第二类：经济发展与建设

经济增长 · 改革开放 · 贸易 · 数字经济 · 平台经济 — 考频持续上升 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

核心词汇

专有名词 / 术语

经典翻译句子模板（5句）

1. 改革开放以来，中国经济经历了飞速发展，数亿人口摆脱了贫困。
Since the reform and opening-up, China's economy has undergone rapid development, lifting hundreds of millions of people out of poverty.
2. 中国已成为世界第二大经济体，在全球经济中扮演着越来越重要的角色。
China has become the world's second-largest economy, playing an increasingly important role in the global economy.
3. 电子商务和数字经济的蓬勃发展极大地改变了人们的消费习惯和生活方式。
The booming development of e-commerce and the digital economy has greatly transformed people's consumption habits and lifestyles.
4. 供给侧结构性改革是中国经济实现高质量发展的关键举措。
Supply-side structural reform is a key measure for China's economy to achieve high-quality development.
5. 共同富裕是中国式现代化的重要特征，需要逐步缩小城乡和区域差距。
Common prosperity is an important feature of Chinese-style modernization, requiring the gradual narrowing of the urban-rural gap and regional disparities.
💡 翻译技巧：经济类翻译常用 "undergo rapid development"（经历快速发展）、"play an important role in"（发挥重要作用）、"promote/facilitate"（促进）、"contribute to"（有助于）、"achieve high-quality development"（实现高质量发展）。

第三类：社会发展与民生

人口 · 城镇化 · 社会保障 · 就业 · 扶贫 — 高频常考 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

核心词汇

专有名词 / 术语

经典翻译句子模板（5句）

1. 随着城镇化进程加快，越来越多的人口从农村迁移到城市。
With the acceleration of urbanization, an increasing number of people have migrated from rural areas to cities.
2. 人口老龄化是中国社会面临的一大挑战，需要完善的社会保障体系来应对。
The aging population is a major challenge facing Chinese society, which requires a comprehensive social security system to address.
3. 近年来，政府采取了一系列措施来缩小城乡差距，改善民生。
In recent years, the government has adopted a series of measures to narrow the urban-rural gap and improve people's livelihoods.
4. 精准扶贫政策的实施帮助数千万农村贫困人口摆脱了绝对贫困。
The implementation of the targeted poverty alleviation policy has helped tens of millions of rural poor shake off absolute poverty.
5. 乡村振兴战略旨在促进农村经济、文化、生态的全面发展。
The rural revitalization strategy aims to promote the comprehensive development of rural economy, culture and ecology.
💡 翻译技巧：社会类翻译注意 "aging population"（人口老龄化，不是old population）、"targeted poverty alleviation"（精准扶贫，不是 precise poverty）、"rural revitalization"（乡村振兴，官译）。

第四类：教育与人才培养

义务教育 · 高等教育 · 高考 · 留学 · 素质教育 · 文化自信 ⭐⭐⭐

核心词汇

专有名词 / 术语

经典翻译句子模板（4句）

1. 中国政府高度重视教育，实行九年义务教育制度，确保每个孩子都有接受基础教育的机会。
The Chinese government attaches great importance to education and implements a nine-year compulsory education system to ensure that every child has access to basic education.
2. 近年来，越来越多的中国学生选择出国留学，以拓宽视野、提升自身竞争力。
In recent years, an increasing number of Chinese students have chosen to study abroad to broaden their horizons and enhance their competitiveness.
3. 高考是中国最重要的人才选拔考试，每年有数百万考生参加。
The Gaokao is China's most important talent selection examination, with millions of candidates taking it each year.
4. 提倡素质教育，培养学生的创新精神和批判性思维能力，已成为教育改革的重要方向。
Advocating quality-oriented education and cultivating students' innovative spirit and critical thinking ability have become an important direction of educational reform.

第五类：科技进步与创新

人工智能 · 5G · 高铁 · 数字经济 · 新媒体 · 航天 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

核心词汇

专有名词 / 术语

AI & 数字科技专项词汇

经典翻译句子模板（5句）

1. 中国在高铁、5G通信和人工智能等领域取得了举世瞩目的成就。
China has made remarkable achievements in areas such as high-speed rail, 5G communications and artificial intelligence (AI).
2. 移动支付在中国的普及程度极高，人们只需一部手机就可以完成日常生活中的大部分交易。
Mobile payment is so widespread in China that people can complete most daily transactions with just a smartphone.
3. 人工智能正以前所未有的速度改变着社会生产和生活方式，已广泛应用于医疗、教育、交通等领域。
Artificial intelligence is transforming social production and lifestyles at an unprecedented pace, and has been widely applied in areas such as healthcare, education and transportation.
4. 短视频和直播带货已成为中国数字经济的重要组成部分。
Short videos and livestreaming e-commerce have become important components of China's digital economy.
5. 中国自主研发的北斗导航系统已实现全球覆盖，为各国用户提供精准的定位服务。
China's self-developed Beidou Navigation System has achieved global coverage, providing precise positioning services to users worldwide.
💡 翻译技巧：科技类注意术语准确性：AI第一次出现写全称 "artificial intelligence (AI)"；"big data" 不可数不加s；"virtual reality (VR)" 注意缩写格式。常用动词："transform/revolutionize"（变革）、"apply"（应用）、"develop"（研发）、"promote innovation"（推动创新）。

第六类：生态环境与绿色发展

碳中和 · 碳达峰 · 可再生能源 · 环境保护 · 新能源车 — 热门新增 ⭐⭐⭐⭐

核心词汇

专有名词 / 术语

碳中和 & 新能源专项词汇

经典翻译句子模板（6句）

1. 中国政府提出了"绿水青山就是金山银山"的发展理念，大力推进生态文明建设。
The Chinese government has put forward the concept that "lucid waters and lush mountains are invaluable assets," vigorously promoting ecological civilization.
2. 中国承诺力争在2030年前实现碳达峰，在2060年前实现碳中和。
China has pledged to peak carbon emissions before 2030 and achieve carbon neutrality by 2060.
3. 垃圾分类已在许多中国城市推广实施，旨在减少环境污染，促进资源回收利用。
Waste sorting has been implemented in many Chinese cities, aiming to reduce environmental pollution and promote resource recycling.
4. 中国新能源汽车产销量连续十年位居全球第一，已成为全球最大的新能源汽车市场。
China's new energy vehicle (NEV) production and sales have ranked first globally for ten consecutive years, making it the world's largest NEV market.
5. 可再生能源——包括太阳能、风能和水电——在中国能源结构中的占比持续提升。
Renewable energy — including solar energy, wind power and hydropower — accounts for an increasing share of China's energy mix.
6. 应对全球气候变化需要世界各国的共同努力，《巴黎协定》为全球气候治理提供了基本框架。
Combating global climate change requires joint efforts of all countries, and the Paris Agreement provides a basic framework for global climate governance.
💡 翻译技巧：生态类核心区分："carbon peak"（碳达峰，peak作动词到达峰值）、"carbon neutrality"（碳中和，不是 carbon neutral 作名词用法）、"new energy vehicle (NEV)"（新能源汽车标准译法）、"ecological civilization"（生态文明，中国特色表述的固定翻译）。
